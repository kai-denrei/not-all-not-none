# Fault line 04 — Gender equality

## Framing in Japanese context

Japan's own gender-equality record is contested (it ranked 125th of 146 countries in the WEF 2023 Gender Gap Index), so the Japanese debate cannot easily take an *enlightenment-from-above* tone toward Muslim societies. Japanese-language coverage of Islamic gender questions is largely descriptive (Sakurai on Iranian women, Tanada on second-generation Japanese-Muslim girls' education) rather than polemical. The 2022–2023 Mahsa Amini coverage is the partial exception.
