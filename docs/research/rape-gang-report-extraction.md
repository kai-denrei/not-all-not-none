## CRITICAL PROVENANCE NOTE
This is **NOT** the official government inquiry. It is *The Rape Gang Inquiry
Report*, chaired by **Rupert Lowe MP**, privately crowdfunded (~20,000 donors,
"amplified by Elon Musk"), **non-statutory** ("lacked statutory powers"),
released June 2026. The official **Independent Inquiry into Grooming Gangs** is a
separate body: chair **Baroness Anne Longfield**, statutory, £65m, announced
9 Dec 2025, draft ToR March 2026, ~3-year remit (reports ~2029). The two must
never be conflated on the page.

## 4. Institutional failures (page-cited)
- Police: ignored reports; criminalised victims (arrested, called "prostitutes,"
  charged with perverting the course of justice); accepted a gang member as a
  victim's "appropriate adult" (Michelle p.34); a call handler told a mother "You
  can't describe them as Asian men because that's racist" (p.30); "two-tier
  policing" (p.203); force-specific Appendix III pp.203–205.
- Social care: children's homes as "trafficking hubs"; staff handed children to
  taxi drivers (Lilly p.69–70); returned children to abuse sites (Marlon's
  daughter pp.45–47); records shredded (p.101); pp.207–211.
- NHS: recorded STIs/pregnancies/injuries in children then discharged them back
  to abusers with no safeguarding referral; pp.205–207.
- Schools: saw older men at gates, excluded victims rather than safeguard;
  pp.211–212.
- Taxi licensing: renewed permits for drivers forming "logistical backbone";
  pp.212–213.
- CPS/justice: no single offence captures organised CSE; sentences "4–12 years";
  racial/religious aggravation "never once… invoked against anti-white rape
  gangs" (p.140); pp.137–141.
- Government/politics: report assigns Labour "primary responsibility for the
  longest and most deliberate cover-up"; cites electoral reliance on Muslim
  voting blocs; names Sadiq Khan, Jess Phillips, Andy Burnham, Keir Starmer
  (pp.141–148). The Dec 2025 official inquiry (Longfield) is characterised by the

- Victim-centred justice; statutory ISVAs; sentencing escalation (life starting
  point; 50yr ringleaders / 25yr participants; aggravating factors); deport
  foreign-national convicts; reverse presumption of child consent in grooming;
  **statutory duty to record/publish ethnicity, nationality, religion** of
  victims and perpetrators; criminalise official inaction driven by "community
  tensions"; single "Childhood Sexual Exploitation Act"; "Sammy's Law" (expunge
  victims' coerced criminal records); mandatory frontline training on the
  ethnic/religious pattern; NHS safeguarding duty; prohibit sharia marriages.
  (pp.159–178.)

## 7. Caller note on character & reliability
Advocacy/campaign report, not a statutory inquiry. Headline figures are largely
extrapolations or name-analysis estimates the report itself acknowledges as
imprecise while arguing the true scale is higher. Central claims (250,000; "8
theological aspects"; 95%) rest on contested secondary sources (Lord Pearson;
McLoughlin's *Easy Meat*; Dr. Taj Hargey; Dr. Mark Durie). Some testimony
includes extreme, unverified allegations. Appendix III names institutions but
redacts most individual names. On the page: attribute figures to the report and
its cited sources rather than stating them as independently established fact, and
carry the report's own caveats through.
