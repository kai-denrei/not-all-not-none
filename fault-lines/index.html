How to read these pages. Phase 1 is the structural skeleton: each page steelmans both extremist-Islamist and liberal-democratic positions on the fault line, cites real friction cases in the EU, US, and Japan, names Muslim reformers and inside-critics worth reading on the specific issue, and gathers 4–8 credible sources. Where source-finding still has gaps, pages flag them with HTML comments rather than fabricating citations. Phase 2 will deepen each page into long-form prose with primary-source close readings and policy analysis. The hub essay (linked above) is the manifesto these pages develop in detail.
